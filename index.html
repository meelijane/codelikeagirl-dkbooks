What is computer coding?

Just like humans speak in languages, for example: English, Italian or Chinese - computers speak in programming languages (e.g. Python, Javascript and Ruby on Rails). Coding is giving instructions to a computer in the language it understands (code). Despite all the intelligent and smart things that computers can do they are actually very dumb. They are just very very good at following instructions.
